Join our team. We're hiring!

Our Culture

A self-directed, smart work environment

OrbiSky believes in progress and innovation and in cultivating talent. We hope to work with people who are self-directed and passionate about their achievement.

We want to change the world

Progress and innovation drive our company, and we are always open to new ideas, new solutions, and new opportunities to grow. Our work goes beyond entrepreneurship: we see real opportunities to change the world, improve communities, and make workplaces safer, more efficient, and overall better.

Autonomy and innovation

The environment in our company has a “work hard, work smart” approach, where we expect people to take the initiative and organize their own processes.

Diversity Matters

Committing to diversity

We are aware that the tech field has a history of exclusion, and we are committed not to let this happen here. Our company welcomes people from all backgrounds, and we provide accommodation and support for religious, disability, and any other reasons that might require it.

A safe workplace

We know that diversity drives innovation and are committed at building a fair workplace with no tolerance for harassment. Our policies and culture are directed at creating a safe and inclusive environment where people can comfortably be themselves.

Internships and Educational Opportunities

Who is invited to apply?

We welcome applications from interns from any and all backgrounds, both students and graduates. We commit to diversity and inclusion at all levels.

What do we offer as a company?

OrbiSky hopes to provide a learning environment with real work experience using and implementing high-tech solutions. As a company, we are spearheading AI and machine learning solutions in a variety of sectors. We partner with a variety of institutions to offer internship opportunities.

Reach out today to learn more about the opportunities we have!
