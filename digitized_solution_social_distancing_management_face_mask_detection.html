A Digitized Solution of Social Distancing Management, and Face Mask Detection

Request a Demo

Many organizations are finally re-opening, even as the pandemic continues. This presents unique challenges for businesses, as they are required to follow standard operating procedures or SOPs as set by the WHO and other health organizations, as well as local authorities. The most common requirements involve:

Wearing face masks

Maintaining a safe distance

Reducing the number of people within indoor spaces

This can be difficult to implement, especially because it requires continuous monitoring and an instantaneous response to ensure not only that there is compliance but that employees and clients are not infected.

Smart solutions can minimize the costs and human resources required to maintain these guidelines. We offer a low-cost, cutting edge product that will allow business to stay open safely.

Our Product

We provide an innovative technological solution that can monitor COVID-19 safety guidelines in real time, using integrated edge AI cameras to measure social behavior and reduce the risk of exposure. The solution provides in-house IoT systems and analytics.

What does it do?

Our solution automates the process of monitoring and correcting social behavior for COVID-19 prevention.

What does it accomplish?

  • Reduced costs
  • Quick and easy implementation
  • No need for extensive monitoring or inspection carried out by people
  • No human error
  • Real-time, around the clock monitoring
  • Violations are addressed as they happen

How does it look in practice?

We offer three main functions for COVID-19 prevention.

Face Mask Detection

The device automatically detects and indicates people who are not wearing a mask using red shapes. Anyone concerned can be immediately notified and asked to correct this behavior.

Social Distancing Management

The second main function is identifying whether team members or employees are following proper social distancing (6 feet) or if they are closer, also indicated by green or red shapes. When violations are detected, the individuals can be immediately contacted and asked to correct this behavior.

People In, People Out

The third function we offer is tracking the amount of people in a space, when they leave and enter. This can be used to ensure that the room does not become too crowded and complies with occupancy guidelines (e.g., no more than 30% of a roomâ€™s capacity).

What technology do we use?

We use integrated edge artificial intelligence cameras. Edge is an alternative to cloud that offers significant advantages. Most data today is processed in the cloud, so it is sent back and forth across a server. This has significant limitations: processing delays and the need for a complex infrastructure. Edge offers faster processing that happens at the source, for instance, within the device or close to it, not in a centralized cloud.

Edge computing can power new devices that require fast processing, for example, smart cars, and it represents a new age in computer technology. It reduces latency - delay in data processing due to the interaction between the cloud and the device.

What does using an edge AI mean?

An edge AI runs AI locally through edge computing. This increases the speed at which data is processed and means that you can use AI algorithms with no need of Internet connection and obtaining real-time data.

What are the advantages of using an edge AI?

Edge computing offers significant benefits for the user:

  • Real-time, fast data processing and reduced lag times
  • Lower costs
  • Lower network traffic
  • High efficiency and responsiveness
  • Decentralized processing that reduces the risks of failure and data loss
  • Accessibility for remote locations or devices that need to operate offline:
    • Higher data security and compliance with current regulations
    • Easy to manage and self-contained

What is included with our product?

We provide a Jetson Nano device with camera included. We can install it in your facilities as required. Additionally, we offer free training for any staff members who will be engaging with the product.

Conclusion

Our product serves as an automation device for ensuring compliance with health policies and COVID-19 prevention guidelines. The device provides automatic monitoring, real-time data processing, and an automated response protocol.

Why is this process worth automating? It cuts down on costs and does not interfere with business processes. There is no need to appoint valuable personnel to monitoring and tracking whether people inside the facilities are following prevention guidelines. It gives this duty to an AI that is more efficient and can provide 24/7 tracking for face masks, social distancing, and number of people.

Businesses are currently facing many challenges due to the pandemic. Many of these can be minimized by using smart solutions, and automating social distancing and health can create a more efficient, more productive, and safer workspace for everyone at a low cost.
