We believe in creating a productive future, a future that makes the most out of technology to free people to be creative. We use cutting-edge technologies combining AI and edge computing to create multi-purpose solutions.

What can this technology do?

Monitor for specific changes, incidents or behaviors

Evaluate distance and number of people in a space

Take immediate action

Using edge computing along with AI provides significant advantages. Unlike other approaches, it can:

  • Be installed in remote locations or places with bad connection
  • Keep the data more secure
  • Respond in real time with no lag
  • Automate more processes
  • Reduce costs and use of human resources

Real-life applications

Manufacturing

Manufacturing is slowly becoming more and more automated. Edge AI devices can take this further and deeper, delegating tasks like quality evaluation, immediate correction, and failure prevention. Addressing issues as they occur thanks to a decreased latency can help avoid accidents.

If a machine starts overheating, an AI can shut it down before a fire starts or before it breaks down.

Healthcare

In regards to healthcare, this can be used to prevent infection, ensure adequate compliance with guidelines, real-time patient data processing, and improved decision-making.

If a person enters a room without wearing a mask, they can immediately be detected and asked to correct this behavior.

Agriculture

For agriculture, edge computing is perfect because it does not require an ongoing connection to the cloud. Smart farms can use AI for monitoring weather or temperature and reacting if something is not adequate.

If the temperature rises too much, the AI can lower it before it damages the plant.
