Privacy Policy

We, OrbiSky Systems Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "OrbiSky Systems" or "we"), understand that facial technical services require data such as direct and indirect user portraits, which is interconnected to user privacy and requires a prudent approach to protection. Therefore, we are obligated to recognize, respect and protect all user privacy.

The Company's Privacy Policy (hereinafter referred to as "Policy") is intended to describe the way in which OrbiSky Systems and its subsidiaries collect, use, disclose, and protect information. The Policy also provides users with channels to access the personal information the Company holds on them. The Policy does not apply to personally identifiable information that users provide to the Company via other means or that it has accumulated through other public sources.

By using this website, The Company will deem users to have accepted this Policy and consented to its collection, storage, usage, disclosure, sharing and transfer of their details in accordance to the provisions provided in this Policy. The Company will amend the Policy sporadically to update incomplete or outdated information, or information that requires modification. Major Policy changes will be published on the Company's official website, and new policies will take effect immediately following the release.

Ⅰ. Collection of Personal Information

When users (including through the products you connect with) utilize the Company's services, it collects their information, which includes personal and non-personal data, for the purposes and processes defined in the Policy.

Personal information refers to the data associated with identifying unique individualities in a person. This is combined with other information, including but not limited to the user's name, date of birth, mailing address, telephone number, email address, payment info, and videos containing personal information, etc.

Non-personal information refers to non-specific data that cannot be directly linked to any one individual, such as occupation, languages spoken, zip codes, area codes, serial numbers, URL, automatically recorded access data (such as browser norms, domain names, page views, referral pages, IP addresses, and page navigations). It also includes mobile device preferences and unique identifiers, videos exempt from personal information, country and time zone information revealing where the product has been accessed, the location, the mobile phone operator identifier(OI), and the equipment software platform and hardware information and so on. The Company combines non-personal information with personal information received from the users, which it treats as personal information on a combined basis.

Ⅱ. Collection of Information from Third-Party Sources

The Company reserves the right to collect user information from open market sources (to the extent permitted by law), which it may use in conjunction with other information related to the user or received from them. Should the Company use this information in combination with the user's personal information, the information will be considered to be "personal information" for the duration of its combined handling.

Ⅲ. How We Use Cookies

To personalize the online experience for users and make it easier for them to access the website, The Company will direct small data files, called cookies, to the user's computer or mobile device. Cookies assigned to the user are unique and can only be read by Web servers in the domain where the cookies have been published. The Company sends cookies to its users to simplify repetition during logins, and store their shopping preferences or the items in their shopping cart. This is combined with other data to provide users with their unique shopping preferences, which helps them optimize their advertising preferences and enables them to determine their login and account or data security status.

The Company does not use cookies for any purpose other than those described in the Policy. Users can manage or delete Cookies according to their preferences, and they can clear all cookies saved on their computers easily. Although most web browsers will automatically accept cookies, users can typically modify their browser settings to reject them if that is their desire. In addition, they can also clear all cookies stored on the software.

Ⅵ. Protection of Personal Information

The Company has taken customary safety measures to protect the personal user information it is provided with. However, no method of transmitting or storing electronic data is completely secure and the Company cannot provide assurances that such information will never be accessed, used or distributed in a manner that violates the Policy.

In the process of providing services, the Company may obtain public facial information through search technology, social networks, public databases, etc. It may obtain non-public face information through the data submitted by the users. The Company guarantees that any non-public information or data submitted directly to it from users will be treated as private information, which will not be disclosed to any third parties. The data collected and submitted in any of the Company's applications will only be used for that specific application and will not be accessed or used by any other application. The Company invites and encourages application developers, its users, and any third parties to offer suggestions on facial information security to ensure that the privacy of app developers and end users is kept confidential.

The Company protects the privacy of minors in accordance with applicable laws. It encourages minors, those under the age of 18, to visit its platform or utilize its services under the supervision and guidance of their parents.
